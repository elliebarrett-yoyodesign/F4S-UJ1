Screen annotations

Each screen in the journey is described below. Select a screen name to jump to it.

  • New component
  • Repurposed component
  • Existing content
  • Conversion point

  1. 1 Landing page, audience selector

    The entry point for this journey. A visitor identifies as wanting to understand the impact of their gift and selects the matching card in Zone 2 of the Impact Hub landing page. Both the landing page and this card are already built, see the linked wireframe in the page header. Zone 2's rationale from the strategy deck: improve onward journeys for each audience, ensure each audience sees relevant impact information, and show where money goes regardless of gift size.

    Already delivered

  2. 2 Condition-filtered impact stories

    A new sub-page that lets visitors browse impact stories by eye condition, mirroring the existing A to Z. Makes content relevant to visitors arriving with a specific condition in mind, and gives search engines condition-specific pages to index.

    New component

  3. 3 Scale of VI in the UK

    Reuses the existing stats page to show how many people are affected by vision impairment nationally, building the case for the donor story that follows.

    Existing content

  4. 4 Donor story

    A named case study linked to the condition the visitor has been browsing, giving a Protector or Believer donortype a direct, personal connection before they're asked to give.

    Existing content

  5. 5 Lottery players branch

    A short explainer on what a lottery entry funds. This path is shorter than the main one and doesn't route through the donate widget, it ends at lottery sign-up.

    Existing content

  6. 6 In Memory branch

    An explainer on giving in memory of someone, linking through to funded research for their condition first, then rejoining the main path at the donate widget.

    Existing content

  7. 7 Contextual donate widget

    The same repurposed component as Zone 5 of the landing page, reused here with copy that adapts to the condition the visitor has been reading about. Leads to two conversions: a one-off donation or the Vision Circle regular giving scheme. Carries Zone 5's rationale from the strategy deck: show where money goes regardless of amount, and leverage urgent need to donate.

    Repurposed component
